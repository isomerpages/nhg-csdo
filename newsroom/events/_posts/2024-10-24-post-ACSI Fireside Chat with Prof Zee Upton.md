<p>On 24 October, faculty members and clinicians gathered for an enriching
evening with <strong>Prof Zee Upton</strong>, Deputy Vice-Chancellor (Research
&amp; Innovation) at the University of Newcastle, Australia. Moderated
by <strong>Dr Joseph Lo</strong>, Head and Senior Consultant of Vascular
Surgery Service at Woodlands Health, the Fireside Chat provided a unique
platform for an open and engaging dialogue on research and innovation in
healthcare.</p>

<p>Drawing from her extensive experience in both Singapore and Australia,
Prof Upton provided compelling insights into the comparative strengths
and differences in the mentality, ecosystem, and policy support of both
countries. She shared how these factors uniquely shaped the research and
innovation landscape of both countries.</p>
